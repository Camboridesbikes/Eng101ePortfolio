What's So Special Here?

Well, for starters I am coding this entire website from scratch! This site is not only my writing portfolio, but it is also part of my web developer portfolio. Talk about being efficient.All the photos I will be uploading to the site are from yours truly. That’s right, I’m a photographer too!

 

If you haven’t already discovered: this website is a platform to present a writing portfolio for an English composition course from Arizona State University through edX. It is an Eng101 course and the first college-level English course I have ever taken. I will present three essay projects as you can see above, along with twenty shorter journal entries that can be accessed from their own ‘Writing Journal’ page.The Journals will be updated often throughout the next seven weeks, while the writing projects will be more periodical.

 

As you proceed through the site some content may not be there yet; and as I am coding the entire thing from scratch, there may be design aspects I will update from time to time and I am surely going to make a mistake from time to time. So please have patience with me and if you find any mistakes in my writing or in my pages, bring it to my attention through the edX discussion forums or email me at hattendorfcameron@gmail.com
